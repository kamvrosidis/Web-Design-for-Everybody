Final Project

Dog walking is a popular activity for dog owners, providing both physical and mental stimulation for their canine companions. It involves taking a dog on a walk, typically on a leash, for exercise and socialization. Dog walking can take place in various settings, such as parks, neighborhoods, or nature trails. The duration and intensity of the walk can be adjusted based on the dog's breed, age, and energy level.

Dog walking offers numerous benefits for both dogs and their owners. For dogs, it helps maintain their physical health by preventing obesity and promoting cardiovascular fitness. It also provides mental stimulation, reducing boredom and anxiety. Socialization during walks allows dogs to interact with other dogs and people, improving their social skills and reducing aggression. For owners, dog walking provides an opportunity for exercise, stress relief, and a chance to connect with nature and their furry friends.
